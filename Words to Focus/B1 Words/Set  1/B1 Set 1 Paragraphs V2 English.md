# Daily Life and Habits

---

## 1. The Morning Starts Early

My daily routine begins every morning at six o'clock. I start the day with a cold shower in the bathroom. Usually the shower lasts about ten minutes. Afterward, I brush my teeth in front of the mirror. My habit is to make coffee in the kitchen every morning. My mother's habit is different — she prefers to drink tea. I eat breakfast at the table in the dining room. My brother is still sleeping because he has a different habit. He doesn't start his day until eight o'clock. We have different habits in our family. I often forget to charge my phone — that's also a bad habit. My father reminds me of it regularly. Today I remind myself to be on time. Daily life in our house is calm and organized. I leave the house at half past six. So my daily routine starts very early. My sister says the early routine is healthy. I go to work and think about today's schedule. The schedule today is very full and busy. I hurry so that I don't arrive late.

---

## 2. The Schedule and Appointments

My schedule for this week is very full. On Monday I have an important doctor's appointment. I organized the appointment two weeks ago. It's hard to get a doctor's appointment. My mother helps me organize the appointments. She also organizes the appointments for my father and brother. We organize the appointments together on Sunday. I write the schedule in a small notebook. My boss gave me a new appointment for Tuesday. I postponed the old appointment because I was sick. Sometimes you have to postpone appointments — that's normal. But I don't like postponing appointments. I also postponed the arrangement with my cousin. The arrangement was planned for Saturday. We are now planning a new arrangement for next week. Unfortunately, I forgot the dentist appointment. I forget appointments when I'm very busy. My grandfather sometimes forgets his appointments too. I regularly remind him of his weekly doctor's appointment. Punctuality is very important in Germany.

---

## 3. Work at the Office

I work as a computer scientist in a large office in the city. The office is modern and has many large windows. I start my work daily at eight o'clock. My boss is very punctual and expects punctuality from everyone. The punctuality of my colleagues is usually very good. I sit at my desk next to a friendly colleague. We work together daily on various projects. At ten o'clock we take a short coffee break. The break lasts about fifteen minutes. After the break we start working again. The stress in the office is sometimes very great. With a lot of stress I try to take a short break. I complete many tasks on the computer daily. I always complete the most important tasks first. Sometimes a task takes longer than planned. I completed two big projects today — that makes me happy. My boss reminds us of the monthly reports. I always prepare the monthly report on Thursday. I look forward to the end of the workday every day at five o'clock. After work I relax at home.

---

## 4. After Work and Free Time

The end of the workday begins for me at five o'clock in the afternoon. After work I often go into the garden. Free time is very important for my health. I like to spend my free time with family. I usually spend the evening on the sofa in the living room. My dog lies on the carpet next to the sofa. We watch TV together — that's our daily habit. My daughter prefers to spend her free time with her friends. She occasionally spends the weekend at her grandmother's. Free time belongs to us — that's very important. I relax after work with a good book. My husband relaxes by cooking in the kitchen. He enjoys cooking and prepares dinner for the family. After dinner we all relax together. Sometimes we take a break from TV and play a game. Free time on the weekend is especially nice. I always plan the weekend free time in advance. On Saturday we have planned a get-together with friends. I look forward to the free time on Sunday. After work is the best part of my daily routine.

---

## 5. Planning the Weekend

The weekend is very special for my family. We always plan the weekend together on Friday. My son really enjoys planning the weekend in advance. We occasionally spend the weekend at my parents' place. The weekend at the grandparents' is always nice. My grandmother prepares a lot of food for the weekend. She prepares the meals with a lot of love. I help my grandmother prepare lunch. On Saturday we go to the supermarket to shop. We also like to spend the weekend in the garden. In the garden we relax and play with the dog. My brother sometimes forgets to come to the weekend meal. I then remind him by phone. On Sunday we start the day slowly and calmly. We have a long breakfast together at the big table. Unfortunately the weekend only lasts two days. Sometimes I wish the weekend would last longer. I also do small tasks around the house on the weekend. I plan the schedule for the next week on Sunday afternoon. The weekend ends at midnight — then daily life begins again.

---

## 6. Shopping and the Supermarket

Every Tuesday I go to the supermarket to shop. Shopping is a regular activity in my daily routine. I prepare a list so I don't forget anything. Without a list I always forget something important. For example, I often forget the milk or the bread. My wife reminds me to take the list with me. The supermarket is large and has many different departments. I usually finish the shopping in thirty minutes. Sometimes it takes longer because there are many people. When in a hurry I only buy the most important things. In the rush I sometimes forget things from the list. My daughter occasionally comes with me to the supermarket. She helps me organize and carry the groceries. We buy fresh vegetables and fruit daily. The fruit is next to the vegetables in the supermarket. I usually pay at the checkout with my card. Sometimes I wait a long time at the checkout — that causes stress. The delay while shopping sometimes annoys me. I always try to be back home on time. Shopping is an important activity for the family.

---

## 7. Family and Daily Life at Home

Our family has five people: father, mother, son, daughter, and me. We live in a large house with a beautiful garden. The house has four bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a large kitchen. In the living room there is a large sofa and a TV. My daughter has a small room next to the bathroom. Daily life in our family starts early in the morning. Everyone has their own activity and their own schedule. My husband's activity is to get up early and cook. My son does his homework daily after school. He does his homework at the table in the dining room. My mother visits us regularly on the weekend. She then spends a lot of time with her grandchildren. The family likes to relax together in the evening. We organize a monthly family dinner with everyone. The monthly family dinner is a nice habit. I remind all family members of the meal via message. The children always wait impatiently for the big meal. My grandfather likes to tell old stories during the meal. We really enjoy spending time at family gatherings. Family is the most important thing in my daily life.

---

## 8. Hobbies and Activities in Free Time

In my free time I have many different activities. My favorite activity is reading — I read daily. I usually read in the bedroom on the bed. My husband has a different activity — he likes to paint. He spends his free time creatively with colors and brushes. Our daughter occasionally rides a horse. The horse is called Bruno and is very large and brown. We visit the horse regularly on the weekend. My son is interested in computers and games. He spends too much free time in front of the TV. I remind him to also play outside. In spring we regularly go for walks in the park. In summer we like to spend our free time in the garden. In autumn and winter we read a lot and relax. Free time activities are very important for wellbeing. I plan the leisure activities for the week in advance. Sometimes I postpone my hobbies because of stress. I try to regularly find time for my activities. Without free time and activities daily life would be very boring. My hobbies help me forget the stress.

---

## 9. Travel and Holidays

On holidays we like to spend time away from home. We usually plan the holiday weeks in advance. I always start planning trips early. My wife organizes the trips very well and punctually. We occasionally spend holidays in Switzerland or Austria. Once we spent a holiday in Tunisia — that was wonderful. The trip to Tunisia took ten hours. We prepared the trip together with our family. Packing the suitcases is great fun for my children. I always handle the important travel documents myself. I usually wait patiently at the airport for our flight. Sometimes there is a delay with the flight — that is unpleasant. Once the delay lasted almost three hours. During a long delay I try to relax. I read a book or drink coffee during the waiting time. My children sometimes become impatient and loud during delays. While traveling I forget daily life and stress. The holiday always ends too quickly — we return refreshed. After the holiday I like to remember the beautiful moments. Traveling is the most enjoyable activity in my free time.

---

## 10. Stress, Punctuality, and Relaxation

Stress is unfortunately a big part of modern daily life. I usually get stress at work or with the schedule. Dealing with stress — that is an important skill. I try to schedule short breaks daily. The break helps me clear my head. After a short break I start fresh again. Punctuality helps me avoid stress. With good punctuality daily life runs much better. My boss regularly praises my punctuality. I remind my children daily of the importance of punctuality. Late people sometimes cause me stress. A colleague's delay once shifted our entire schedule. I always hurry in the morning so that I am on time. But too much rushing in the morning also causes stress. With great stress I pause and breathe deeply. In the evenings I relax with my family on the sofa. I like to relax with quiet music or a book. My husband relaxes by cooking after work. We try to manage the stress of daily life together. An organized daily routine with breaks, punctuality, and free time makes life better.
