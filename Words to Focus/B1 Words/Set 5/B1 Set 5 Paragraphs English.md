## Paragraph 1 – My Apartment

I live in a small but comfortable apartment in Hamburg. The apartment has a living room, a kitchen, a bedroom, and a bathroom. In the living room, there is a large sofa next to the television. On the table lies my schedule for the week. I am very hardworking and always start my daily routine on time. Every morning I get up at six o'clock and shower in the bathtub. After the shower, I have breakfast in the kitchen. The refrigerator is always full of fresh vegetables and milk. I sit at the table and eat slowly because I enjoy the morning. My habit is to drink a coffee before work. Breakfast usually lasts twenty minutes. Afterward, I leave the apartment and walk to the stop. I take the tram to work every day. The ride is comfortable and only takes fifteen minutes.

---

## Paragraph 2 – The Journey Begins

Today I am planning a trip to Vienna. I have already booked the ticket online. The ticket is not expensive – it only costs ninety euros return. I pack my suitcase with warm clothes because it is cold in autumn. The luggage is not heavy, but the suitcase is big. At the train station, I read the departure board and look for my platform. The train departs punctually at eight thirty. I sit in a comfortable seat next to the window. The journey takes approximately four hours. I explore on the map which sights I want to visit. Vienna is a beautiful city with a lot of history. I am especially looking forward to the trip to Schönbrunn Palace. We travel directly to Vienna without changing trains. The journey is pleasant and I relax.

---

## Paragraph 3 – At the Airport

My sister is traveling to Tunisia next week. She is flying from Hamburg Airport. The flight is direct and takes three hours. She has already reserved her ticket, but the plane is almost fully booked. At the airport, she checks her luggage and drops off her suitcase. The luggage weighs twenty kilograms – that is the maximum. She waits at the gate and reads a book. The plane is unfortunately delayed – it has a delay of one hour. My sister is not happy, but she stays calm. She calls me and says: "I'm fine, but the flight is delayed." I tell her: "Stay calm, you will arrive on time." She reaches Tunisia in the evening. The hotel is beautiful and right by the sea. She stays there for seven nights.

---

## Paragraph 4 – Planning a Vacation

My husband and I plan a vacation every summer. This year we would like to travel to Spain. We have not booked a hotel yet, but we search for deals every day. The available hotels are either very expensive or fully booked. I order information online about various excursions. My husband prefers to book directly at the travel agency. He is going to the travel agency tomorrow to organize the trip. We need two tickets – one for him and one for me. The train tickets to the airport are cheap. We always pack our suitcases one week before the trip. The luggage must be light because the plane has a limit. I often forget the charging cable – that is my bad habit. My husband always reminds me. We are very much looking forward to the vacation.

---

## Paragraph 5 – Daily Life at Work

I work as a teacher at a school in the city center. My daily routine begins at seven o'clock in the morning. I leave the house on time and walk to the stop. The tram usually comes every ten minutes. At school, I start my first lesson at eight o'clock. The break lasts twenty minutes and I relax in the staff room. My job is very interesting, but sometimes also stressful. I complete many tasks every day – I teach, correct, and plan. The end of the workday is at three thirty. After work, I go shopping at the supermarket. I buy vegetables, meat, and bread. The cashier is always friendly and kind. I pay with a credit card because I have no cash on me. At home, I cook dinner for my family.

---

## Paragraph 6 – Shopping in the City

On Saturday I go shopping with my mother. We take the bus into the city center. The bus departs directly from the train station and we get off after five minutes. The shop is large and has many offers. I buy a red dress – it is on sale and costs only twenty euros. My mother buys a blue sofa for her living room. The receipt shows the price including VAT. I like to save money and only buy affordable things. Sometimes I order things online because it is cheaper. The customer receives the delivery after two days. I once returned a product because it was spoiled. The shop exchanged it without any problems. We spend three hours shopping. In the end, we are tired but satisfied.

---

## Paragraph 7 – Health and Wellbeing

In winter, I sometimes come down with a cold. I then feel weak and tired. The doctor examines me and prescribes medication. I take two tablets every day and drink plenty of water. The flu is very contagious – my brother caught it too. He coughs and sneezes all day. We both recover slowly, but after a week we feel better. Health is very important for everyday life. I am regularly fit because I go for a walk every day. My body is strong when I sleep well and eat healthily. I avoid spicy and fatty food because it gives me headaches. My doctor says I should eat more vegetables. The pain in my back is sometimes chronic, but I do exercises to counter it. With the right treatment, one can recover quickly.

---

## Paragraph 8 – Family and Home

My family is large and very nice. There are six of us – my parents, my brother, my two sisters, and me. My father is a doctor and my mother is a teacher. We live in a large house with a garden and a terrace. In the dining room there is a long table with six chairs. On weekends we cook together and eat as a family. My grandmother also comes and always brings cake. She bakes very well and her cake always tastes delicious and sweet. My little brother is nine years old and he is very funny. My older sister is married and has a son. We visit her occasionally on holidays. The family outing is always a special experience. Last summer we all traveled together to Austria. The trip was comfortable and we visited many sights.

---

## Paragraph 9 – Cooking and Eating

I love cooking and learn new recipes every week. Today I am cooking a delicious dish with vegetables and meat. I peel the potatoes and cut the meat into small pieces. Then I fry everything in the pan with oil and spices. The food smells wonderful and my son comes curiously into the kitchen. I taste the sauce and season it with salt and pepper. The recipe also recommends fresh herbs – I use parsley. I serve lunch at one o'clock. For dessert there is fresh fruit – apples and pears. My daughter prefers sweet desserts, but today there is only fruit. The main course tastes excellent – not too salty, not too spicy. My husband eats two portions because it tastes so good. After eating, we aid our digestion with a walk in the garden. Cooking is a creative activity for me.

---

## Paragraph 10 – Return and Memories

After vacation I always come home happy. My plane lands at the airport on time. I collect my luggage and take a taxi home. The suitcase is full of souvenirs for the family. My children wait excitedly for me and ask: "What did you bring?" I hand out the gifts and tell them about my trip. I explored many cities and visited many new things. The most beautiful excursion was a boat ride on the lake. I took many photos, which I show to the family. My son asks: "Where are we traveling next year?" I say: "Maybe to Germany or to Switzerland." We are already planning the next vacation and I book early so that we get good prices. The return ticket is cheaper when you reserve it early. Traveling is my greatest passion and I always look forward to the next trip.
