### ⚠️ Notes on skipped or limited forms:

- **verzögern**: Personal conjugation (ich verzögere, du verzögerst…) was not used naturally since in travel context this verb is almost always used passively or impersonally (_Der Flug wurde verzögert / Das Gepäck hat die Abfahrt verzögert_). Active personal forms like _ich verzögere_ would be unnatural here.
- **der Urlaub**: Plural (_die Urlaube_) was skipped — while grammatically possible, it is very rarely used in natural spoken or written German. Genitive (_des Urlaubs_) appears in one sentence.
- **das Gepäck**: Has no natural plural in German (it is an uncountable mass noun like "luggage" in English). All uses are singular.
- **hin und zurück**: Functions as a fixed phrase/adverbial rather than a fully declinable adjective. It appears in ticket contexts and is not inflected; comparative/superlative are grammatically impossible.
- **einfach**: In the travel sense of "single ticket," this usage is more limited than its general meaning of "easy/simple." Both meanings were incorporated where natural.
