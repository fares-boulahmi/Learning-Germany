# Deutsche Texte – Set 7: Weather & Nature

---

## Paragraph 1 – The Weather and the Morning

This morning I wake up early and look out the window. The weather is cool and cloudy, but the sun shines a little between the clouds. I feel a bit cold because the window is open, so I close it quickly. The sky is gray and there are many clouds, but it isn't raining yet. My sister is having breakfast in the kitchen and she says: "It's very windy outside today!" I hear the wind blowing and I can see that the trees are moving strongly. The temperature is maybe ten degrees, so it's not hot, but not very cold either. I take a warm shower and put on warm clothes. After breakfast I leave the house and immediately feel the cool wind. The wind blows strongly from the left and my hair quickly becomes messy. I walk to the bus stop and wait for the bus. It starts to rain lightly, so I open my umbrella. The rain isn't heavy, but it dampens my coat a little. I think: "Today is a typically rainy autumn day." Luckily the bus comes on time and I quickly get on.

---

## Paragraph 2 – A Thunderstorm in the Afternoon

In the afternoon I'm working in the office and suddenly the sky becomes very dark. The clouds are now black and stormy, and I see the first lightning far away. Then I hear the thunder loudly and all my colleagues look out the window. "A storm is coming!" says my boss. The thunderstorm is very strong and the rain begins to fall heavily. Lightning flashes again and again and after each flash the thunder follows quickly. The temperature drops rapidly and it gets cold in the office. We close all the windows and wait until the storm passes. The wind blows so strongly that a tree in front of the office bends dangerously. My colleague says: "I'm scared of the lightning, it's dangerous!" I remind him that we are safe in the office. After an hour the thunder stops and the rain grows weaker. The sky is still cloudy, but the worst of the storm has passed. The sun isn't shining yet, but it's no longer so stormy. We relax and drink coffee during the break.

---

## Paragraph 3 – The Garden in Spring

In spring our garden is beautiful. The flowers bloom everywhere and the tree next to the balcony is green again. My mother goes into the garden every morning and waters the plants. She plants new flowers next to the house and enjoys the nature. The temperature is warm, about twenty degrees, and the sun shines almost every day. It sometimes rains at night, but during the day the sky is sunny and blue. I help my mother in the garden and we plant vegetables next to the flowers. The water from the garden hose dries quickly on the warm ground. The flowers grow very fast in the warm weather and with plenty of rain. An old flower unfortunately wilts because it gets too little sun. My father repairs the fence in the garden and says: "Spring is the best season!" I agree with him, because nature in spring is so lively and colorful. In the evening we sit on the terrace and enjoy the warm air. The wind blows gently and brings the scent of the flowers to us. It is a perfect spring evening for the whole family.

---

## Paragraph 4 – Snow in Winter

In January it often snows in Germany. I wake up in the morning and see white snow everywhere – on the roof, in the garden, and on the street. The snow is fresh and the sky is clear and cold. The temperature today is minus five degrees, so it is really cold and I freeze immediately when I open the door. My brother and I take the dog to the park and we walk through the deep snow. The dog is very excited and runs through the snow very fast. The sky is light blue and the sun is shining, but it doesn't warm enough. It's no longer snowing, but the wind blows cold and brings small snowflakes from the trees. The trees are completely white and nature looks like a winter fairy tale. I photograph the beautiful nature with my phone. My brother builds a snowman next to the tree and I help him. We spend two hours outside and then we are very cold. At home we drink hot tea and eat cookies in the warm kitchen. I look out the window and see how the snow slowly melts because the sun is shining more strongly. The next morning the ground is wet and the snow is almost gone.

---

## Paragraph 5 – A Hot Summer Day

In July it is often hot in Tunisia and also in Germany. Today the temperature is thirty-five degrees and the sun shines all day. The sky is sunny and there are no clouds – perfect summer weather! I get up early because it is already warm in the morning. After breakfast I go with my family to the lake. The water is cool and we swim for a long time. The sun shines on the water and it glitters beautifully. My daughter picks wild flowers on the shore and asks: "Why do the flowers wilt so quickly?" I explain that the hot sun and the lack of water cause the flowers to wilt. We water the flowers with lake water, but it doesn't help much. In the afternoon it is so hot that we sit in the shade of a large tree. The tree is old and large and it protects us well from the strong sun. A light breeze blows and it feels very pleasant. We eat our lunch under the tree and relax. In the evening we drive home and the temperature becomes cooler. I take a cold shower and feel fresh and fit afterwards. It was a wonderful, hot summer day.

---

## Paragraph 6 – Fog in the Morning

In autumn there is often fog in the morning. Today the sky is gray and everything is foggy – I can barely see the street. The temperature is cool, about eight degrees, and the air feels damp. I leave the house at seven o'clock and drive carefully to work. The fog is very thick and I drive slowly. The trees along the road look mysterious in the fog and the leaves fall slowly to the ground. In the office everyone says: "What a foggy morning!" My colleague asks: "Will the sun shine today?" I answer: "Maybe in the afternoon when the fog is gone." Around ten o'clock the sun begins to shine and the fog slowly lifts. The temperature rises to fifteen degrees and it becomes pleasantly warm. The sky is now partly cloudy, but it isn't raining. Nature looks fresh and clean after the fog. I take a short walk during my lunch break and enjoy the fresh air. The flowers in the park are growing well because the soil is still moist from the fog. It is a typical autumn day – first foggy and cool, then sunny and warm.

---

## Paragraph 7 – Rain and Shopping

It rains all day today and I still have to go shopping. I take my umbrella and leave the apartment. The rain isn't very heavy, but it wets everything – the street, the trees, and the flowers in the garden. I go to the supermarket and buy vegetables, meat, and bread. At the checkout I pay with my credit card because I have no cash. The cashier gives me the receipt and I quickly leave the supermarket. Outside it is still raining and the wind almost blows my umbrella away. The temperature is cold – only six degrees – and I freeze in my thin jacket. I hurry and walk quickly home. At home I dry my wet things and hang them on the heater. The rain stops in the evening and the sky becomes clearer. I look out the window and see that the trees and flowers are glistening after the rain. Nature looks fresh and green. I cook soup for dinner because it was so cold and rainy. My husband comes home and says: "The weather was really bad today!" We eat together and enjoy the warm evening at home.

---

## Paragraph 8 – Wind and Nature in the Park

On Sunday I like to go to the park, even when it's windy. Today a strong wind is blowing and the trees are moving back and forth. The temperature is pleasantly cool, about fourteen degrees, and the sun shines occasionally between the clouds. I sit on a bench next to a large tree and read a book. The wind blows the leaves from the trees and they fall to the ground around me. Some flowers in the park are already wilting because it is autumn. Other flowers are still blooming in beautiful colors – red, yellow, and orange. A child runs through the park trying to catch the falling leaves. The sky is alternately cloudy and sunny and the light changes constantly. It starts to rain briefly, but the rain quickly stops again. After the rain, nature smells wonderfully fresh. I continue walking through the park and see an old man watering flowers. He is planting new flowers next to the fountain and explains to me: "These flowers grow well, even in cool weather." I find that interesting and ask him the names of the flowers. We talk for a long time about nature and the weather. It is a beautiful, windy autumn afternoon.

---

## Paragraph 9 – The Weather While Traveling

Last summer I traveled to Germany. I booked my ticket and packed my suitcase. At the airport I found out that the weather in Germany would be cool and cloudy. After arriving, the temperature was only seventeen degrees – much cooler than in Tunisia, where it had been hot. It rained lightly on the first day and I had to buy an umbrella. On the second day the sky was sunny and I explored the city. I saw many beautiful flowers in the parks that were just blooming. The trees were large and green and nature was wonderful. On the third day there was a storm – there was lightning, thunder, and heavy rain. I stayed in the hotel and postponed the excursion. The storm lasted only a few hours and afterwards the sky was clear again. I went on a trip into nature and took beautiful photos of the trees and flowers. The wind blew gently and the temperature was pleasantly warm. On the last day the sun shone all day and I enjoyed my vacation very much. The trip was wonderful, even though the weather was sometimes stormy. I fondly remember the beautiful nature in Germany.

## Paragraph 10 – Seasons and Family

In our family, everyone has a favorite type of weather. My mother loves spring, when the flowers bloom and the temperature becomes warm. She goes into the garden every day and waters her plants with joy. My father likes summer, when the sun shines and it is hot. He works in the garden and plants lots of vegetables among the flowers. My brother finds winter the most beautiful, because he loves skiing when it snows. He never freezes because he always wears warm clothes. I personally love autumn the most, when the sky is cloudy and the wind blows. The leaves of the trees turn colorful – red, yellow, orange – and nature looks wonderful. As a family we spend a lot of time outdoors in nature, no matter what the weather is like. In summer we go on trips to the lake when the sun is shining. In autumn we go to the park when it is rainy and cool. In winter we visit the Christmas market, even when the fog is thick or it is snowing. In spring we work together in the garden and plant new flowers and trees. The weather connects our family and reminds us of beautiful shared moments. Nature is an important part of everyday life for all of us. We are grateful for all four seasons – the sunny summer, the windy autumn, the cold winter, and the blossoming spring.
