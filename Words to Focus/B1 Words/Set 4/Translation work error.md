## 🔴 Most Frequent Errors

**1. Verb conjugation & position** This is your biggest issue. German has strict verb-second (V2) word order — when a sentence starts with anything other than the subject, the verb must still come second.

- ❌ `Gestern , ich mache eine Portion` → ✅ `Gestern machte ich eine Portion`
- ❌ `Jeden Morgen , ich frühstücke` → ✅ `Jeden Morgen frühstücke ich`

Also, conjugations are often wrong for the subject:

- ❌ `Wir probiere` → ✅ `Wir probieren`
- ❌ `Kann du` → ✅ `Kannst du`

**2. Adjective endings (declension)** You frequently forget to adjust adjective endings based on gender, case, and article:

- ❌ `ein kleiner Nachtisch nach dem Mahlzeit` → ✅ `einen kleinen Nachtisch nach der Mahlzeit`
- ❌ `leicht Nachtisch` → ✅ `leichten Nachtisch`
- ❌ `frische Gemüse` (after mit) → ✅ `frischem Gemüse`

**3. Article gender and case** You often use the wrong article or ignore case entirely:

- ❌ `nach dem Mahlzeit` (das Mahl → `nach der Mahlzeit`) → ✅ `nach der Mahlzeit`
- ❌ `mit meiner Schwester` ✅ — this one you got right actually
- ❌ `zu mein Hause` → ✅ `zu meinem Haus`

**4. Tense consistency** You mix present and past tense mid-paragraph, especially in narrative contexts:

- In Paragraph 1 you switch between present (`koche`) and past (`mache`, `sagt`) — pick one and stay consistent. Past narratives should use Perfekt in spoken/written informal German.

**5. Spelling & typos**

- `zusamen` → `zusammen`
- `zusage` → `ich verspreche / ich versichere`
- `werten` → `warten`
- `bake` → `backe`
- `durstest` → `durstig`
- `schart` → `scharf`

## 🟡 Structural / Grammar Gaps

**Subordinate clause word order** — you don't yet show confident use of `dass`, `weil`, `ob` clauses with verb-at-end structure. This will be essential at B1+.

**Preposition + case pairing** — you know some prepositions but haven't locked in which ones take accusative vs. dative:

- `vor die Hauptspeise` → ✅ `vor der Hauptspeise` (dative for position/time)
- `zu mein Hause` → ✅ `zu meinem Haus`

**Missing articles** — you drop articles entirely sometimes:

- ❌ `Hast du gutes Rezept` → ✅ `Hast du ein gutes Rezept`
- ❌ `täglich Hauptspeise` → ✅ `die Hauptspeise des Tages`
