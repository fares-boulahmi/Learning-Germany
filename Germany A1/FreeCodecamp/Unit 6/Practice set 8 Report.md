- Statement: Wir **dürfen** hier telefonieren. (subject → verb)

#### **ERROR 5: Preposition Confusion — bis/um, in/bei, am/um**

**Frequency:** 6+ occurrences

**Pattern:** Mixing up prepositions that serve different functions:

|Confusion|Your Version|Correct Version|
|---|---|---|

**Rule summary:**

- **in** = in (places with articles): in einem Büro
- **bei** = at (companies, people): bei BMW, bei mir

**Set 9 intervention required:**
