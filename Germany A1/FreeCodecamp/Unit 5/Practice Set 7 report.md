### Section 1.3 — Answering "Wann" Questions: 4/6 🟡

**Error 1:** "Um fünfzehn nach acht frühstücke ich" — The expression "fünfzehn nach acht" is not standard German. The correct form is **"Viertel nach acht"** (quarter past eight). 8:15 is always "Viertel nach acht", not "fünfzehn nach acht". The 24-hour official clock says "acht Uhr fünfzehn", but the conversational form uses "Viertel". Both exist, but "fünfzehn nach" as a hybrid does not.

**Error 2:** "Um halb dreizehn esse ich" — "halb dreizehn" does not exist in German. "Halb" only works with 1–12 (the clock face). 12:30 is **"halb eins"** (halfway to one). Never "halb dreizehn".
