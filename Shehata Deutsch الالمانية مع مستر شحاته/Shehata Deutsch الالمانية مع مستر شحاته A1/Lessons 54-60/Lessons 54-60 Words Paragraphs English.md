# A Free Day with Hobbies

Today is Saturday, and I have a day off. I wake up at eight o'clock and feel great. I'm in a wonderful mood because I have no stress. The weather is sunny and warm — perfect for an outing. I eat breakfast in the kitchen and drink coffee. Afterward, I brush my teeth and shower. I plan to do many things today. Everyday life can be stressful sometimes, but on weekends I can relax. I am hardworking and want to make good use of my free time. This day off should truly be a lovely one.

---

After breakfast, I go for a walk. I go for a walk every Saturday — it's my habit. The park is large and green, and the air is fresh and mild. I should spend at least an hour outside today. Many people are jogging or running in the park. A man with a dog runs past me. I photograph the trees and flowers — photography is one of my favorite hobbies. I love taking photos because it's creative and calming. With my camera, I take many beautiful pictures. Nature in spring is truly stunning and colorful.

---

At noon, I meet my friend Anna. We meet regularly on weekends. Anna is kind and funny — I enjoy spending time with her. She asks: "What are you up to today?" I reply: "I'd like to go to the cinema. Would you like to come along?" She says: "Of course! I'd love to!" We plan to go to the cinema at two o'clock. The cinema isn't far — we walk there. The film starts at a quarter past two. I'm very excited because I love movies.

---

Before the cinema, we go to a café. The café is small and cozy, right next to the cinema. We sit at a table by the window. Anna orders tea, and I order coffee. We talk about our families and our hobbies. Anna mentions she still has to cook this evening — her mother is coming to visit. I tell her that I enjoy swimming and cycling. "How often do you cycle?" she asks. "I cycle weekly, usually on Sundays," I say. The break at the café is very pleasant.

---

At the cinema, we watch a funny film. The film lasts nearly two hours. We laugh a lot — it's really comedic and entertaining. After the film, we head out of the cinema. "I'm sorry, I have to go home now," says Anna. "Unfortunately I need to cook for my mother." I say: "No problem! We'll meet again next week." We say goodbye — "Bye! See you later!" The afternoon was wonderful. I walk home slowly and enjoy the sunny weather.

---

At home, I want to exercise. I should be active every day — that's important for your health. I put on my running shoes and go jogging. I jog occasionally in the afternoon when I'm free. Jogging isn't easy, but I'm fit and strong. After thirty minutes I'm tired and sweaty. I drink plenty of water, because it's good for the body. Then I shower and put on fresh clothes. Exercising makes me happy and relaxed. The body always feels better after sport.

---

In the early evening, I cook. Cooking is one of my hobbies — I love to cook creatively. I stand in the kitchen by the stove and prepare the food. In the fridge I have vegetables, cheese, and eggs. I make a vegetable stir-fry — it's simple and delicious. The kitchen smells warm and inviting. I eat alone at the table in the living room. The food tastes wonderful — I'm truly satisfied. After eating, I tidy up the kitchen and do the washing up. A good dinner makes the day complete.

---

After dinner, I sing a little. Singing is my favorite hobby — I sing every day. I sing loudly and happily in the living room. Sometimes I dance along too — dancing makes me happy. My brother finds it funny, but he doesn't like to sing. I should still study my vocabulary this evening, but first I sing! Music is very important to my leisure time. I sing in German and in Arabic — that's my after-work routine. At eight o'clock I stop and sit down on the sofa. The evening is calm and beautiful.

---

On the sofa, I read a book. Reading is a quiet and enriching hobby. I enjoy reading in the evenings because it's relaxing. The book is about a journey through Germany and Switzerland. I would love to travel to Germany one day — that's my big dream. The book contains many interesting facts about the country. I read for an hour, then I grow tired. The book rests on the table beside the sofa. I remind myself to continue reading tomorrow. Reading before bed is a very good habit.

---

At ten o'clock, I go to bed. Good night! This day off was truly perfect. I took photos, went for a walk, cooked, sang, and read. I met my friend and watched a film. Free time should always be this lovely and active. Tomorrow is Sunday — I plan to go cycling and take a trip somewhere. I'm content and tired, but happy. The body needs rest after such an active day. I close my eyes and fall asleep quickly. A free day filled with hobbies is truly the best.
