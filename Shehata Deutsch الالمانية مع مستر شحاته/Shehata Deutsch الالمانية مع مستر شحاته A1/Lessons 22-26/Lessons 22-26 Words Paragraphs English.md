## Paragraph 1 – The Apartment and the Family

The man lives in a small apartment in the city. The apartment has a bedroom, a bathroom, a kitchen, and a living room. In the kitchen stands a large refrigerator next to the stove. On the stove, the woman cooks coffee every morning. The woman drinks the coffee from a blue cup. The table in the dining room is brown and large. Next to the table stands a chair for the father. The son sits on the sofa and reads a book. The daughter sits next to the son and watches the television. The dog sleeps under the table. The cat sits on the shelf next to the window. The window is large and the garden outside is green. In the garden grows a red rose and a white lily. The mother loves the flowers in the garden very much. The father works in the office and comes home at eighteen hundred hours. The parents eat together with the children at the table. The man's brother also lives in the city. The sister lives in a house with a large garden. The house has a garage and a balcony. On the balcony stand many flowers in colorful shades. The grandmother visits the family every Saturday. The grandfather comes by car. The family is large and friendly. The husband and wife have been married for twenty years. They have three children – two sons and a daughter. The oldest child is nineteen years old. The family's apartment is clean and beautifully decorated. On the wall hangs a photo of the whole family. Next to the photo hangs a colorful picture. The lamp above the table gives off warm light. The wardrobe in the bedroom is large and made of wood.

## Paragraph 2 – The Morning and the Daily Routine

The man wakes up every morning at six o'clock. He gets up at six thirty and goes to the bathroom. In the bathroom, he first brushes his teeth. Then he showers with warm water. The shower is next to the bathtub. The towel hangs next to the sink. He dries off and gets dressed. The woman has breakfast in the kitchen. She cuts an apple and a banana. On the table stands a bottle of milk and a glass of orange juice. The children also have breakfast with the mother. The boy eats cookies and drinks milk. The girl eats a bread roll with jam. The mother makes coffee with the coffee machine. The coffee smells very good in the morning. The father leaves the house at seven o'clock. He takes the car and drives to the school. He is a teacher at a large school in the city. The teacher rides her bicycle to the university. She is young, hardworking, and very intelligent. The students arrive at school at eight o'clock. The clock on the wall shows exactly eight o'clock. The teacher writes with a pen on the board. The students write with pencils in their notebooks. The folder lies on the student's desk. The ruler and the scissors lie next to the folder. The girl's report card is very good – she is very hardworking. The boy sometimes forgets his book at home. The teacher is friendly and explains everything again. The classroom has twenty chairs and ten tables. On the wall hangs a map of Germany. The school also has a large library.

The professor's office is next to the classroom. On the shelf stand many books and a photo album. The monitor displays the engineer's program. The woman goes to the supermarket on Tuesday. The supermarket is next to the bakery in the city. At the bakery she buys fresh rolls and bread. The butcher shop is next to the bakery. The family eats at the restaurant every Friday. The table at the restaurant is reserved for five people. Next to the restaurant there is a mosque and a church. The mosque is white and very beautiful. ## Paragraph 6 – The Weather and the Seasons

The weather in Germany is often changeable. In winter it is cold and sometimes it snows. The children love the snow in winter. In spring the weather is warm and the flowers bloom. The rose in the garden blooms in April and May. In summer the sun shines almost every day. The sun rises early and sets late. The moon is very bright at night in summer. In autumn the weather becomes cooler and it often rains. The umbrella stands next to the door of the house. The mother takes the umbrella with her when it rains. Today there is a thunderstorm with a lot of rain and wind. The thunderstorm begins at noon and ends in the evening. The children stay home during the thunderstorm. The father drives slowly by car during the thunderstorm. The climate in Germany is moderate and not extreme. In the north it is often cooler than in the south. The capital Berlin is located in the north of the country. Munich is in the south, close to the mountains. The mountains are very beautiful and white in winter. The Earth revolves around the sun in one year. Spring begins in March and ends in June. Summer lasts from June to September. Autumn brings colorful leaves in red, yellow, and orange. Winter begins in December and lasts until February. The clocks move forward one hour in spring. In autumn the clocks move back one hour. The weather greatly influences people's moods. When it is sunny, people are friendlier and happier. When it rains, many people prefer to stay at home. The fan in the office runs all day in summer.

## Paragraph 7 – Travel and Tourism

The family plans a trip to Austria in the summer. Tourism in Austria is very well known. The capital Vienna is a very beautiful city. The father books the hotel at the travel agency in the city. The plane departs at ten o'clock in the morning. The journey takes two hours. In Vienna they visit many museums and churches. The museum in Vienna showcases art and history. The museum director is a friendly Austrian. The tourist from Turkey photographs the church. The tourist from China buys souvenirs in the shop. The actor from Great Britain is also a tourist in Vienna. The family eats at the restaurant next to the museum. The waiter brings the bill after the meal. They pay by card and return to the hotel. The hotel has a swimming pool and a garden. The garden has many flowers and a small fountain. The children play in the hotel garden. The mother reads a newspaper on the balcony. The father takes photos of the city with his camera. Vienna is also well known for its music and culture. The culture of the city is rich and interesting. The son buys a book about the history of Vienna. The daughter buys a necklace as a gift for the grandmother. The grandfather receives an album as a memento of the trip. The trip is wonderful and the family is very happy. On the last day they also attend a concert in the city.
