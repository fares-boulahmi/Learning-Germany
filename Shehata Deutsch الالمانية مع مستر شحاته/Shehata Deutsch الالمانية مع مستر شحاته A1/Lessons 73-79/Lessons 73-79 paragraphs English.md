Here is the English version:

---

Every morning I wake up early. I wake up at six o'clock and get up immediately. My dog is already waiting beside my bed. I go to the bathroom and brush my teeth. Then I shower and get dressed. My room is small but nice. I feel healthy and fit today.

---

After getting up, I go to the kitchen. I boil water and start making breakfast. Today I'm baking a small cake for my family. My mother helps me in the kitchen. She gives me the ingredients and shows me the recipe. We chat while cooking. The breakfast tastes good to us.

---

After breakfast, I tidy up the room. I sweep the floor and clean the windows. I wash the laundry in the washing machine. Then I hang it up — in the garden or on the balcony. Later I take it down and fold it. Ironing takes an hour. I never forget to take out the trash.

---

In the morning I go grocery shopping at the supermarket. I bring my mother fresh vegetables and also take some meat along. At the store I pay by credit card. The prices are good today — many items are on sale. I save some money and don't spend it all. The shopkeeper recommends a new product to me. I order it for next week.

---

At noon I cook lunch. I chop the vegetables, peel the potatoes, and fry the meat. My brother helps me cook — he loves seasoning the food. We serve the meal in the dining room. The whole family sits at the table. The food tastes delicious and fresh. No one goes hungry in our house.

---

In the afternoon I relax a little. I watch TV for an hour or call my friend. Sometimes I draw or paint a picture. My father reads the newspaper and looks out the window. My sister listens to music and thanks me for the cake from the morning. We trust each other in our family. Free time belongs to us — I enjoy it very much.

---

Toward evening I wash the pots and clean the kitchen. My mother kindly tells me to clean the table too. I answer her politely and follow her wishes. She trusts me and believes I am hardworking. I sometimes forget things, but she always forgives me. I promise her to get up earlier tomorrow. She smiles and thanks me.

---

My little brother is sick today. He is coughing and sweating a lot. My mother examines him and gives him a tablet. The medicine doesn't taste good to him, but he takes it. I bring him water and hand him the bottle. He is weak and tired, but he is slowly recovering. I wish him good health.

---

In the evening my aunt gives me a magazine and a letter from Germany. I read the letter and will reply to her soon. She often sends us news and stories from her homeland. I trust her very much and believe her words. My father shows us pictures from our last outing. We fondly remember those beautiful days. The family chats together for a long time in the evening.

---

Finally I prepare myself for sleep. I brush my teeth once more and turn off the light. I lie down in bed and relax. My dog sleeps beside me on the carpet. Tomorrow a new day begins again — I'm already planning what I need to get done. Everyday life is sometimes stressful, but I feel healthy and content. Good night — I'm falling asleep now.
